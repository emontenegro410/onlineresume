Indiana University Bloomington

OurCS Conferance & Workshop

HelloResearch(OurCS) was a three day research-focused workshop providing opportunities for undergraduate women to work on exploratory problems in teams led by researchers from academia and industry.

Over the weekend, I worked on the research topic: Designing Hospital Information Systems of the Future: Connecting Patients, Providers, and Family. Although hospital care is carefully documented and that information is electronically available to clinicians, few information systems exist for patients and their families to use while they are in the hospital. Information often appears trapped within the hospital room. In this research project, I use Human-Centered Computing research and design techniques to envision the hospital information systems of the future for young patients who have been diagnosed with critical conditions. By transforming qualitative findings into design requirements, and propose audacious design solutions using interactive prototyping toolkits I was able to experiance a unique weekend next to extordanary people.
